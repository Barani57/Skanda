Our Story

A Home Away From Home, In God's Own Town

Sri Skanda Bhavan has welcomed pilgrims and families to Thirukkadaiyur for over two decades. Located steps from the sacred Amirthakadeswarar Temple - one of the Paadal Petra Sthalangal - we offer clean, comfortable accommodation and pure South Indian meals prepared with devotion.

Whether you come for Sashtiapthapoorthi, Shanthi Homam, or to simply seek peace, Sri Skanda Bhavan is your home.
